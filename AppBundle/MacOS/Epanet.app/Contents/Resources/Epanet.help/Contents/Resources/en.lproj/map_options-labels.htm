Map Options - Labels

 Top  Previous  Next

The Labels page of the Map Options dialog form controls how labels are displayed on the map.

 

Display Labels

Displays map labels (labels will be hidden unless this option is checked).

 

Use Transparent Text

Displays text with a transparent background (otherwise an opaque background is used).

 

At Zoom Of

Selects minimum zoom at which labels should be displayed; labels will be hidden at zooms smaller than this unless they are meter labels.
