Energy Report

 Top  Previous  Next

EPANET can generate an Energy Report that displays statistics about the energy consumed by each pump and the cost of this energy usage over the duration of a simulation.

 

To generate an Energy Report select Report >> Energy.

 

The Table page of the resulting report lists the following statistics for each pump in the network:

·Percent Utilization (percent of the time that the pump was operating)

·Average Efficiency

·Average power consumption (kw-hr) per million gallons (or cubic meters) pumped

·Average rate of energy usage (kwatts)

·Peak rate of energy usage (kwatts)

The total cost and demand charge (if any) are listed at the bottom of the table.

 

The Chart page of the report displays a bar chart that compares a statistic selected from the list above for each pump.
